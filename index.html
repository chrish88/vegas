Welcome to the outskirts of Las Vegas

When most people think of Las Vegas, they think of casinos with slot machines, nightclubs with party people, and the strip with lots of alcohol. There is, however, much more beauty and adventure far awary from the neon lights. The purpose of this site is to show you some of the cool places that Las Vegas has to offer. If you get a chance to step outside the city limits, you will find a marvelous and breathtaking landscape.
